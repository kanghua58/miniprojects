Unit 1 - QUIZ TIME!

Congratulations on making through all the lessons in Unit 1!

In addition to the small tests at the end of every 8 lessons, there will always be one major test at the end of every unit. These unit tests will cover things that you learned from the previous 25 lessons of the unit.

They may be difficult, but that is okay! Difficult is good! Nobody learns anything by doing something easy.

There are three sections to this test.

  1. Comprehension
  2. Grammar
  3. Listening (Audio transcripts provided after the test)
