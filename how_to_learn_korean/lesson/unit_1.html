UNIT 1: Basic Korean Grammar

Welcome to Unit 1 of HowToStudyKorean.com. My little sister told me that she taught you how to read Korean! That's good, because here in Unit 1 you will start learning basic grammar, words and sentence structures. It is very important when you learn a language to build up your vocabulary to be able to speak in every day conversations. But, because your are just starting out, the first few lessons in this Unit will have a smaller amount of new words than usual. As you progress, lessons will include more and more words to add to your vocabulary!

Unit 1: Lesson 1-8

We will start by introducing basic sentence structure. You will learn about all the irregulars in Korean grammar, and how to use them in sentences. These first 8 lessons will be a strong foundation for your future studies!

Unit 1: Lesson 17-25

In these final 8 lessons of Unit 1, you will be introduced to the most basic connecting particles (~고, ~고 싶다 and ~고 있다 to name a few). You will also learn about using 잘/못 and the many different ways that you can ask questions in Korean, along with the words 이렇다, 그렇다 and 저렇다.
