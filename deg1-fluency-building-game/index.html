Welcome to the Phoneme Catcher Game! Your task is to catch falling words that match the phoneme shown on your basket. Use the left and right arrow keys to move the basket and score points for every correct word you catch. Avoid incorrect words, as they will cost you points. Aim for the highest score by quickly and accurately catching the right words. Enjoy sharpening your phonemic skills!
